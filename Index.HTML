Our Story

Moody Spa has been a cornerstone of the Waltham community for generations. As a family-owned and oriented business, we take pride in being more than just a convenience stop—we are your neighbors.

Whether you're stopping in for daily essentials, visiting us for lottery services, or seeking reliable community resources, you’ll always find a friendly face and a helping hand. Our commitment to supporting our local residents remains at the heart of everything we do.

"Family based, community focused, and always here for you."
